Welcome!

I'm a lead research engineer at Samsung Research Institute Bangalore. I invent technologies for monitoring health and wellness at home using novel sensors and machine learning.

I was associated with Vision and Image Processing Lab, IIT Bombay as an integrated bachelor and master student.

My research interests include computer vision, optics and signal processing.

I enjoy playing music (guitar, drums and piano), mountaineering and photography.
